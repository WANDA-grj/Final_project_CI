I'm moving and can't take my pet with me.

Moving or any change within the family can serve as an excuse. For example, a pregnancy, the birth of a baby or an allergy.
The illness or death of the animal's caretaker may cause the family to get rid of the dog.

Unwanted litters

The main cause of abandonment is uncontrolled litters The females tend to have a couple of hit in a year and can give birth from a couple of puppies to ten.
Lack of owners in controlling females in heat and wandering in search of a female without thinking about the consequences.

It is in our hands to prevent

Yes, it is in our hands that dogs do not end up on the street. So that abandonment is no longer a problem, we can take the following measures:
- Sterilize. Ask your vet to learn about the process.
-Identify so you can recover your dog if he gets lost.
- Reflect before having a pet.
