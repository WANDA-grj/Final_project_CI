Who we are

We are a very new shelter, we started as a group of friends that our passion is animals
and we wanted to do something for those who are abandoned, our vision is to be able to give shelter
to cats and any other type of animals and to be able to provide them with a shelter.
We trust in our mission and our purpose is to take care of the dogs until they can find a home.
We are also looking for people who want to contribute with donations.
